## The problem

A café or shop owner is on the floor all day. Meanwhile their customers are talking everywhere:

- ⭐ A 1-star Google review at 11pm
- 🍽️ "Soğuk geldi" on Yemeksepeti
- 📉 Saturday sales quietly down on the POS

It's scattered across 5 apps nobody has time to open. The pattern hides in the noise.

**They find out something's wrong when the week's numbers drop — too late to fix it.**

---

## Why today's tools don't help

| Tool | Why it fails the owner |
|---|---|
| Google / delivery dashboards | You log in and read everything yourself |
| BI tools (Metabase, Looker) | Built for analysts, not shop owners |
| Review aggregators | Collect, but don't decide or act |

None of them say: **"Send it to my phone and let me decide from there."**

---

## The solution

**Signal: Collect → Understand → Deliver**

1. **Collect** — connect every channel (reviews, delivery apps, POS)
2. **Understand** — AI ranks what matters and writes what to do
3. **Deliver** — the one thing that matters lands on **WhatsApp**

Reply **"1"** for details, **"2"** to mark it handled. No app. No login.

- **History** — every alert sent + the decision taken.

---

## Prototype — how the insight engine works

- **Input:** raw signals (source, content, time, sentiment)
- **AI (Claude):** groups them, ranks importance, writes the action and the projected impact — adapts language to the business type
- **Owner never sees scores or jargon** — only plain words: Urgent / Soon / When you can / Low priority, and "23 customers mentioned this"
- **Smart bit:** cross-source correlation — e.g., *Kadıköy sales down 18% on Saturday morning AND "too slow" reviews up for the same branch → boosted to Urgent*
